For your eyes, Jaan

My confession

My dearest Jaan,

Before you, I thought love was a loud thing — fireworks, grand gestures, words shouted from rooftops. You taught me something truer: love is also the hush when you rest your head on my shoulder, the way you remember the small things, the safety I feel when you simply say my name.

Loving you has been the most honest thing I have ever done. Not because it is always easy — but because even on hard days, my heart still points in one direction. Yours. Always yours.

You are not just my girlfriend. You are my favourite thought in the morning, my soft place to land at night, my partner in laughter and in growing up. With you, I am braver, kinder, and more myself than I ever thought I could be.

If I could give you one gift beyond this page, it would be the certainty I carry inside me: I choose you — not once, but in every quiet decision, every ordinary Tuesday, every future I imagine.

Jaan, I am so deeply in love with you. Thank you for being my today and every tomorrow I dare to hope for.

Forever yours,
with all my love

No matter how many anniversaries we celebrate, I will never get used to how lucky I am that you exist — and that you said yes to me.
